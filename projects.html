Engineering Projects

Reverse Engineering of a Commercial UAV

Aerodynamics XFLR5 Stability Analysis

Analyzed aerodynamic performance and stability of a fixed-wing IC-engine pusher UAV with an inverted V-tail configuration. Utilized XFLR5 for computational analysis to determine lift, drag, and stability coefficients.

Design of Garbage Disposal Unit for Plastic Bottles

Mechanical Design Prototyping Waste Management

Engineered a multi-stage waste management prototype featuring a high-efficiency plastic shredding module specifically designed for pre-crushed bottles. Focused on mechanism design to maximize torque and shredding efficiency.

R&D Composites Manufacturing

Developed at National Innovation Center Nepal. Conceptual design and simulation of a fixed-wing fuselage. Designed and fabricated an aluminum mold via casting for producing the fuselage using expanded polystyrene (EPS).
